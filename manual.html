Core workflow

Build the quote in this order.

1
Choose the lane.Origin and destination apply to the full shipment.
2
Select quote type.Use Full Quote for delivery service. Use Middle Mile Only for freight movement.
3
Add orders.Use separate orders when service levels or freight groups are different.
4
Add measurements.Group identical boxes or pallets together. Add another group when size or weight changes.
5
Review and request.Open the detailed breakdown, add needed services, then send the booking request.
Quote Coach
  • Use the coach before sending. It flags missing pickup dates, liftgate checks, appointment needs, dimensional weight, and White Glove time buffers.
  • Use the buttons. Some coach cards can add a service or jump to the right section for you.
  • Use notes for anything unusual. Access restrictions, timing limits, dock/forklift availability, stairs, and customer instructions make review faster.
  • Custom Review is normal for oversized freight. Over 10 linear feet, the calculator saves the quote details so OKE can confirm equipment and capacity.
Service levels

Choose the handling that matches the delivery.

01CurbsideDelivery to driveway or first dry area. Best for simple handoff when indoor placement is not required.
02ThresholdInside front door delivery. Useful when freight needs to cross the door but does not need room placement.
03Room of ChoiceCarried to the chosen room. Use this when placement inside the home or business is required.
04White GloveUnbox, assemble, and remove debris when selected. Extra time is entered on the White Glove order itself.
05Additional ServicesAdd carrier accessorials for the freight move, plus final-mile extras such as stairs, door removal, haul-away, or appointment when they apply.
06Custom ReviewFor oversized freight, special access, unusual equipment, or lanes where OKE should confirm before pricing.
Measurements

How to enter boxes, pallets, and weights.

Different size or weight?Click Add box group or Add pallet group. Do not average different freight together.
Only know total weight?Open Only have total order weight and enter the order total. Dimensions still calculate cube and linear feet.
Length, width, heightMeasure the packaged freight. Use the longest side as length and floor-to-top as height.
Tip: Use packaged dimensions, not product-only dimensions. Labels, packing slips, and manufacturer specs are usually better than visual estimates.
Before booking

Quick accuracy checklist.

ALaneConfirm city, province, pickup location, and destination location before sending the request.
BFreightConfirm total pieces, pallet count, dimensions, actual weight, and whether weight is per box, per pallet, or total order.
CAccessConfirm stairs, elevator, dock, appointment needs, doorway restrictions, and whether debris removal or assembly is required.
DTimingChoose the preferred pickup date and include any schedule restrictions in the booking notes.
EContactInclude the best name, email, phone number, pickup address, delivery address, and any access instructions for follow-up.
FReviewUse the copy button for a clean summary, or contact OKE directly when the calculator shows Custom Quote.
After request

03ReopenUse Open in calculator when the same quote needs editing, duplication, or a revised request.
